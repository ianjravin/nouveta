Our platform, Node, is an agnostic, omni-channel platform that allows corporates to harness mobile channels to serve their customers effectively.

Secure, simple and agnostic ecosystem

The platform is iterative and agile, and hosts SMS, USSD, mobile money wallets, card payments and virtual payments.

Frictionless user experiences

We are therefore able to develop payments across all these channels, including web and in-app channels, with simple one step authentications that take the hassle out of paying for services and goods.

Low cost channels
